WHY TRUST US?

Evoluzn Inc was established in 2012 in Illinois, USA, focusing on wireless broadband devices, including 5G, 4G, and 3G routers. Renowned for producing high-quality, reliable products, Evoluzn Inc leverages cutting-edge technology and innovative engineering to enhance connectivity and user experience.


In 2019, Evoluzn India Private Limited was founded as a fully owned subsidiary to expand the company's reach in the Internet of Things (IoT) sector. This subsidiary specializes in the Research, Development, and Manufacturing of IoT devices essential for Home, Office, and Industrial automation. The creation of Evoluzn India highlights the company's dedication to driving innovation and offering state-of-the-art solutions to a global market.


By combining the expertise of both entities, Evoluzn Inc and Evoluzn India aim to provide comprehensive, high-quality technological solutions, continually pushing the boundaries of technology to create a more connected and automated world.
